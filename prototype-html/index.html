Buffalo National River

Introduction

The Buffalo National River is one of the first national rivers in the United States and offers a unique natural experience.

Features of Buffalo National River

The Buffalo River is known for its clean water, spectacular scenery, and diverse wildlife. Visitors can explore the river by canoe, hike the scenic trails, and visit historical landmarks.

Wildlife

The park is home to diverse wildlife including deer, elk, and wild turkeys.

History

The river was designated as a National River in 1972 due to its unique ecosystem and cultural history.
